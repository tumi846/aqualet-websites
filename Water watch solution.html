🔧 Key Features

✅ Real-time turbidity monitoring using sensors
📲 Instant mobile alerts via Blynk App
🌐 Wi-Fi enabled ESP32 microcontroller
💡 Low-cost and easy to deploy in homes or schools

⚙️ How It Works

AquaAlert uses an ESP32 microcontroller connected to a turbidity sensor to measure water clarity. When unsafe levels are detected, the device sends real-time data and alerts to the Blynk IoT app on your phone.

You can also view trends, set up thresholds, and receive warnings before using contaminated water.

🌍 Impact on SDG 6

This project directly supports UN Sustainable Development Goal 6 by ensuring access to safe drinking water through:

  • 🚱 Detecting dirty or unsafe water before it's used
  • 📉 Reducing waterborne disease risk
  • 📊 Helping communities track and improve water quality
  • 👨‍👩‍👧 Empowering people with affordable technology
